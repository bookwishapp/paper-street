Saturday Swaps —
trade when you're finished.

Once a month each category gets its own swap Saturday. Bring items, get tickets, take something home. And even if you're not swapping — swap day brings discounts on that week's category, no trades required.

Every Thursday at 7 PM

Thursday Nights
on Paper Street.

Small gatherings every Thursday evening. Each week has a theme. A quiet hour or two with other people who like the same kinds of things you do.

Every Thursday · 7 PM $5 admission Includes one swap ticket Advance tickets required Get tickets

Quiet Reading Night

Bring a book. Read alongside others. No discussion, no agenda — just the company of people who like to read.

Art Night

Bring your sketchbook or whatever you're working on. All skill levels. No instruction required.

Game Night

Board games and card games. We supply games from the shop — bring your own if you have a favorite.

Writers Group

Share writing and get feedback in a low-stakes, friendly setting. Bring a page or just listen.
